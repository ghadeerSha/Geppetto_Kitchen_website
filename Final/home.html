Our Story...

Welcome to Geppetto's Kitchen. We serve the best Italian food, bringing Italian home cooking to the lower mainland. Our door has been opened since 2015. Over the years our kitchen has developed to provide our customers with a refined and delicate cuisine that oozes flavour. At Geppetto's kitchen we take pride in delivering warm and friendly service to your table. Using only finest and freshest of ingredients we provide our dinners with healty meals including accomendation for gluten free, vegetarian and vegan customers. All our ingredients are supplied by Britsh Columbia's very own farmers.
